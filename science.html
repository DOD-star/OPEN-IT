3rd Prep | Second Term Comprehensive Summary

Unit 1: Chemical Reactions

Lesson 1: Chemical Reactions

  • Thermal Decomposition: Compounds break down by heat into simpler elements.
  • Substitution: Simple (Metal + Acid) and Double (Neutralization & Salt/Acid).
  • Redox: Oxidation is losing electrons; Reduction is gaining electrons.

Lesson 2: Reaction Speed

  • Affected by: Surface area, Concentration, Temperature, and Catalysts.
  • Enzymes: Biological catalysts (like Oxidase in Sweet Potatoes).

Unit 2: Electric Energy

Lesson 1: Electricity Properties

  • Current Intensity (I) = Q / t | Unit: Ampere
  • Potential Difference (V) = W / Q | Unit: Volt
  • Ohm's Law: Resistance (R) = V / I | Unit: Ohm

Lesson 2: Cells & Radioactivity

  • Connecting cells in Series (High Voltage) vs Parallel (Low Voltage).
  • Radioactivity: Discovery by Henri Becquerel. Uses in medicine and space.

Unit 3 & 4: Life Science

Heredity (Genetics)

  • Mendel's Laws (Segregation & Independent Assortment).
  • Dominant traits appear in all individuals of the first generation.

Hormones

  • Insulin: Lowers glucose level. | Thyroxine: Food transformation.
  • Adrenaline: Secreted during fear and anger.
