📘 Part 1: New Hello (Main Curriculum)

⚖️ Essential Grammar (Units 7-12)

1. Present & Past Passive (المبني للمجهول)
  • Present Simple Passive: Obj + am / is / are + P.P.
    Ex: These photos are taken by my sister.
  • Past Simple Passive: Obj + was / were + P.P.
    Ex: The ancient city was discovered in 1812.
2. First & Second Conditionals (حالات If)
  • First Conditional (احتمال في المستقبل): If + Present Simple → will + Inf.
    Ex: If we melt the ice, sea levels will rise.
  • Second Conditional (موقف خيالي/مستحيل): If + Past Simple → would + Inf.
    Ex: If I had more time, I would read more books.
3. Past Perfect (الماضي التام)
  • Form: Subject + had + P.P. (يدل على الحدث الأول في الماضي).
  • Key words: After / As soon as + [Past Perfect], Before / By the time + [Past Simple].
    Ex: After he had saved money, he bought the car.

📝 Core Vocabulary (New Hello)

  • Habitats: Coastal, Wetland, Desert, Forest, Rainforest, Arctic.
  • Environment: Renewable energy, Climate change, Fossil fuels, Landfill site.
  • Space: Satellite, Space station, Astronaut, Telescope.
  • Media: Journalist, Editor, Photographer, Newsreader.
Exam Tip: الفرق بين Robbery (سرقة مكان بإكراه) و Theft (سرقة شيء بدون أن يلاحظ أحد).
🔮 Part 2: Hello Plus (Advanced Level)

🚀 Advanced Grammar Focus

1. Reported Speech (المباشر وغير المباشر المتقدم)
  • Statements: حوّل الأفعال للماضي وفك الأقواص بـ (that).
    Ex: "I am flying tomorrow" → He said that he was flying the next day.
  • Orders / Advice: نربط بـ (to + Inf) في الإثبات أو (not to + Inf) في النهي.
    Ex: The doctor told me not to eat fast food.
2. Modal Verbs for Deduction (الأفعال الناقصة للاستنتاج)
  • Sure it's true: Must + Inf / Must have + P.P.
  • Sure it's impossible: Can't + Inf / Can't have + P.P.
  • Possibility (احتمال): Might / Could + Inf.
3. Relative Clauses (ضمائر الوصل المحددة وغير المحددة)
  • استخدام Whose للملكية، و Whom للمفعول العاقل، والفرق بين Where و Which مع الأماكن (لو فيه وصف للمكان بناخد Which).

💎 High-Level Vocabulary & Idioms

  • Biodiversity: التنوع البيولوجي (The variety of plant and animal life).
  • Deforestation: إزالة الغابات (Clearing a wide area of trees).
  • Architectural Heritage: التراث المعماري للمباني القديمة.
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): الذكاء الاصطناعي وأنظمة الروبوتات المتقدمة.
  • Biographies: كتابة السير الذاتية للشخصيات التاريخية المؤثرة.
Advanced Tip: كلمة Sustainable تعني متجدد وصديق للبيئة وطويل الأمد (Eco-friendly & long-lasting).
